No Automatic Followers by Notifications in Sales Orders

Extension to the module No Auto Subscription

Tested on Odoo 10 Community

The app goal is to extend the module No Auto Subscription ('compoze_no_auto_subscribe') in order not to automatically subscribe clients and do not send excess notifications in sales orders / quotations if portal is installed.

Features

  • By default receivers are not subscribed to sales orders if they got a notification
  • Just flag a check box in an email composer to force automatic subscription
